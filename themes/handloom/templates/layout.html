Road to the empowerment

Bima

Unsuccessful Middle-East ride

Coming from a small village roughly 70km from Pokhara, limited education, as there are no constructed roads, transport is very difficult so it involves much walking. Bima's husband had an opportunity to work in the Middle-East. For him to do this, meant taking loans from friends and relatives. Unfortunately when he arrived this proved to be a scam that happens to many people from Nepal. He barely made enough money to exist let alone send money for his family or pay loans.
